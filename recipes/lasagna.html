Lasagna

Description

Make a batch of cheesy lasagna goodness, just like Mamma Mia used to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian Sausage
  • 1/2 c. minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can Tomato Sauce
  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 8 oz. Mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Throw ya sausage, onions and garlic in a pan and heat 'em up
  2. Dump that sauce up in the pan too
  3. Put ya noodles in the pan, in layers, while dumping ya sauce in there
  4. Top it off with ya cheesy wheezy
  5. Now bake it at 350 degrees for like a hour or so, enjoy
